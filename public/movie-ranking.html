The best Movies According to Adrian

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Your name

A breathtaking anime blending romance and fantasy. Its intricate storyline bridges distance and time, leaving you pondering the connections that transcend. A must-watch for its emotional depth and stunning visuals.

The secret life of walter mitty

This film is a journey of self-discovery that ignites wanderlust. With heartwarming humor and stunning scenery, it inspires us to embrace life's adventures and chase our dreams.

Red Dead Redemption

Even though is not a movie... it is an immersive open-world game that brings the Old West to life. Its rich narrative, breathtaking landscapes, and intricate details create an unparalleled gaming experience that captivates and challenges players.
